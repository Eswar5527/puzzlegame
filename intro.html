RULES OF THE GAME:

  1. THERE WILL BE SCORE AND TIMER FOR EVERY STAGE


  2. EVERY PAGE HAS A CLUE


  3. YOU CAN USE CLUE AT EVERY STAGE BUT AT COST OF 10 POINTS FROM YOUR SCORE


  4. FOR EVERY MINUTE YOU TAKE THE SCORE WILL BE REDUCED BY 5 POINTS


  5. BEWARE OF NOTE IN PUZZLES WHICH HAS FURTHER DETAILS OF THAT STAGE
